# Brief Insights

## Sales Performance

* The total sales revenue generated from all orders was ₹33,585.
* Delivered orders contributed the highest share of revenue, generating ₹17,191.
* Shipped orders accounted for a significant portion of sales, indicating strong ongoing order fulfillment activity.

## Product Performance

* Electronics emerged as the highest revenue-generating category.
* Running Shoes generated the highest revenue among all products, making it the best-performing product in the dataset.
* Home category products had the lowest average selling price compared to Electronics and Clothing.

## Customer Insights

* Rohan Gupta was the highest-spending customer based on total purchase value.
* Aarav Sharma was the second-highest contributor to revenue with multiple purchases.
* Premium customers contributed significantly to overall sales, highlighting the importance of customer retention programs.

## Order Status Analysis

* Most orders were successfully delivered, indicating an efficient order fulfillment process.
* Only one order was cancelled, suggesting a low cancellation rate.
* A small number of orders remained in Pending and Shipped states, representing orders still in the processing pipeline.

## Revenue by Category

* Electronics generated the highest overall revenue due to higher-priced products such as Smart Watches and Bluetooth Speakers.
* Clothing contributed strong revenue despite having fewer products.
* Home products generated comparatively lower revenue but maintained consistent sales volume.

## Data Quality Validation

* No duplicate customer emails were found.
* All foreign key relationships were valid, ensuring referential integrity.
* No products contained invalid pricing values.
* No orphan records were detected in orders or order_items tables.

## Business Recommendations

* Increase inventory and promotional efforts for high-performing Electronics products.
* Focus marketing campaigns on premium and repeat customers to improve customer lifetime value.
* Monitor pending and shipped orders closely to ensure timely delivery.
* Explore opportunities to increase sales in the Home category through targeted discounts and bundled offers.
